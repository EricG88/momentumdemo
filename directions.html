Directions to Momentum




From Downtown Birmingham:



Take I-65 South, follow signs South towards Montgomery and get off to the right on Exit 247 Valleydale Rd. Go 1.1 miles and you will reach Highway 31.
Go one more block to the next light and turn right at the Shell on the corner on to CR 275N which is also Old Montgomery Highway.
Go less than one block down and there we are in a large white building on the right.




From The Summit or 280 Area


Head towards the Galleria on I-459 S and get in the Exit 15 Lane to go south toward Montgomery on I-65 S.
Take the next exit (Exit 247 Valleydale Road) to the right and go 1.1 miles and you will reach Highway 31.
Go one more block to the next light and turn right at the Shell on the corner on to CR247N which is also Old Montgomery Highway.
Go less than one block down and there we are in a large white building on the right.
