Tires & Alignment - The Momentum Way


Momentum has the finest equipment available for your tire and alignment needs



    • The Hunter GSP 9700 Road Force Balancer

    • The Corghi Artiglio Master Tire Machine

Follow these steps to order tires online at a great price, and have us do the mounting and balancing.


For the best prices on tires, we suggest TireRack.com.

There you can purchase tires at wholesale price, with no tax. You will pay shipping, but they usually arrive in a day or so.

Tell them you want them to ship to Momentum, they have us on file as a Recommended Installer.

Once delivered, they will email you, then all you need to do is call us to set an appointment to install.

They have our prices for labor listed on their site (a total of $228 for mounting and balancing four tires).

Run - Flats are $40 additional ($10 each).

If you want an alignment to make sure the tires last, let us know when you call for an appointment as it takes a while to do it to our exacting standards.

The price for a 4-Wheel Alignment is $179.
